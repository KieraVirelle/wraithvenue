Lore

Shadows by the Sea

Nestled along the moonlit shores of Shirogane, WRAITH lives in deep purples and shadowed blacks — half dream, half haunting.

WRAITH is home to the Silken Shades, ethereal dancers moving like living shadows beneath incense and dim light. Wrapped in silk and secrecy, each Shade is soft, dangerous, and impossibly alluring.

Sink into shadow, surrender to temptation, and let the night claim you.
