Lore

Shadows by the Sea

Nestled along the moonlit shores of Shirogane, WRAITH lives in deep purples and shadowed blacks — half dream, half haunting.

Setting

Nestled along the moonlit shores of Shirogane, WRAITH is a dark, gothic strip club where the sound of crashing waves mingles with whispered desire. Bathed in deep purples and shadowed blacks, the venue feels half-dream, half-haunting — a place that exists just beyond the veil.

Silken Shades

WRAITH is home to the Silken Shades, ethereal dancers and courtesans who move like living shadows beneath dim lights and drifting incense.

  • Seductive, paranormal elegance — soft, dangerous, and impossibly alluring.
  • Performances blur the line between fantasy and haunting.
  • Wrapped in silk and secrecy; inviting guests to lose themselves in the dark.

Invitation

Sultry, intimate, and unapologetically decadent, WRAITH offers an escape from the mundane — a place to sink into shadow, surrender to temptation, and let the night claim you.
